Office Cleaning

Whether you have a small office space or big, we meet your expectations by making your commercial property or office neat and clean:
1. Daily office cleaners – daily visits or when needed
2. Janitorial supplies, washroom services and hygiene
3. Commercial carpet cleaning and maintenance
4. Hard floor cleaning, buffing, stripping and sealing
5. Industrial cleaning, move in/out deep cleans
6. Commercial office cleaning services in Nairobi

Sofa Set Cleaning

We always start off by Vacuuming the entire Sofa set before the Steam process begins. This is to remove any loose Dirt and Dust particles giving us a Fresh canvas to clean.

Then we will fire up our steam cleaning machine and begin spraying Stain removal and Shampoos that will work the stains out from even the deepest of fibres. All this is done whilst causing no Damage to the fibre in any way whatsoever.

Carpet Cleaning

Nothing is more noticeable than a stained and dingy carpet. We have the equipment and experience to clean carpets and carpet tile floors quickly and effectively. :
We guarantee our work and are available around your schedule. We clean high-traffic carpeted office floors, older and stained residential carpet flooring, and delicate rugs.

Executive Office Cleaning
